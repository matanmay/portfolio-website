Recent Work

Design and Development of Information Systems - Teaching Assistant

Supported courses focused on "Design and Development of Information Systems," where I facilitated lab sessions and evaluated assignments to enhance student learning and satisfaction. Guided students in mastering essential system modeling techniques, including UML, ERD, and DFD, providing them with a solid foundation in information systems design.

Autonomous Vehicle Research Assistant

Developed an advanced real-time remote telepresence system for autonomous vehicle research, utilizing ROS, Python 3, CARLA Simulator, FastAPI, WebSockets, and ReactJS. This system was designed for a Wizard of Oz experiment conducted at the University of Haifa and Ariel University, enabling remote control and monitoring of autonomous vehicles in simulated environments.

Augmented Reality Safety Assistant 

In this thesis project, we aim to design and develop an augmented reality safety assistant to enhance situational awareness and improve safety in various environments. The assistant will leverage AR technology to provide real-time information and guidance to users, helping them navigate complex spaces more effectively.

Chemistry Simulation Research Assistant 

Developed an interactive web application for chemistry education, utilizing NetLogo and NetTango to create engaging simulations and visualizations. This project aimed to enhance students' understanding of complex chemical concepts through hands-on, simulation-based learning tools.
